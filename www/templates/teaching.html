Teaching Assistantships

I have worked as a teaching assistant for a number of psychology courses at the University of Guelph. Most notably, I led a seminar on statistical analysis for a class designed to prepare high achieving students for an honours thesis research course.

Private Tutoring

I offer one-on-one tutoring to students struggling in undergraduate statistics courses. I have worked with a student whose visual impairment made grasping statistical concepts difficult, as well as with mature students returning to school after an extended absence.
